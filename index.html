## So why is it good? - allows people to show things not finished or working - is designed to be a "safe space" - doesn't need to be useful! get creative :) - doesn't need to be code or a jist, could just be an idea or a series of thoughts - highlight is not so much on "being awesome" as it is on learning and pushing forwards/persevering - low bar to entry - encourages creativity - when people are able to explore/create anything will get a greater variety of ideas (genetic algorithms - fake optimisation)
## So why is it hard? - perfectionism - inner critic - feeling like there's no niche or nothing that's going to be helpful - lack of creativity - lack of confidence - low self-esteem - fear of judgement - fear of failure - fear of silence! - no time to create - no-one to create with - no foothold within the community
## Methods - the rose, the thorn and the bud - celebrating ambition/creativity regardless of project outcome - essentially lower the stakes of failure - fail better
